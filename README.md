# Commons Ledger

The Commons Ledger is the open-source blockchain infrastructure 
powering the e-CAN Dollar — a Canadian regulated digital dollar 
built by e-CAN Digital Technologies Ltd.

## What is e-CAN?

1 e-CAN = $1 CAD — backed 1:1 by Canadian Treasury bills and 
insured bank deposits held by a qualified custodian.

e-CAN is structured for full compliance with Canada's Stablecoin 
Act (Bill C-15, Royal Assent March 26, 2026) from Day 1.

## The Token

- **Symbol:** ECAN
- **Standard:** ERC-20 on Ethereum (Phase 1)
- **Architecture:** Fork of Circle's FiatToken 
  (github.com/circlefin/stablecoin-evm)
- **Decimals:** 6 (same as USDC)
- **Reserve:** 100% CAD — T-bills and insured deposits

## Four Functions

- **Buy** — Purchase e-CAN with CAD via Interac e-Transfer
- **Spend** — QR and NFC payments at zero merchant fee
- **Send** — Offline P2P — zero internet required
- **Redeem** — Direct to your Canadian bank account
